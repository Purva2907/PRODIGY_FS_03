Our Story

PanvelEats was born out of a simple idea: to make delicious food easily accessible to everyone in Panvel and surrounding areas.

Founded in 2023 by a group of food enthusiasts, we noticed that while Panvel had amazing restaurants and street food, there was no centralized platform for food delivery. We set out to change that.

Today, we partner with over 50 local restaurants and serve thousands of happy customers every month. Our mission is to support local businesses while providing convenience to our customers.

We believe in the power of good food to bring people together, and we're committed to making every meal special for our customers.
